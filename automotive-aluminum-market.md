<p><p>The automotive aluminum market consists of three main types: cast aluminum, rolled aluminum, and extruded aluminum. Cast aluminum is used for complex shapes and components like engine blocks due to its versatility and strength. Rolled aluminum is typically employed for body panels, offering lightweight properties and excellent surface finish. Extruded aluminum is utilized for various structural components like profiles and frames, providing high strength-to-weight ratios and design flexibility. Together, these types contribute to enhancing vehicle performance and efficiency.</p></p>
