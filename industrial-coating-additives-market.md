<p><p>The industrial coating additives market comprises several key types, each serving a specific function. Rheology modifiers adjust the flow properties and viscosity of coatings for better application. Defoamers eliminate unwanted air bubbles, ensuring a smooth finish. Dispersants improve the distribution of pigments and fillers, enhancing color and stability. Wetting agents promote surface wetting for improved adhesion and leveling. Other additives may include stabilizers and biocides, contributing to overall performance and durability of coating products in various applications.</p></p>
