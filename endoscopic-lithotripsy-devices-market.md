<p><p>Endoscopic lithotripsy devices are categorized into rigid and flexible types based on their design and application. Rigid endoscopic lithotripsy devices feature a stiff construction, providing stability and precision during stone removal but are limited to certain anatomical areas. In contrast, flexible endoscopic lithotripsy devices are more adaptable, allowing for access to various anatomical structures, making them suitable for complex cases. Both types play crucial roles in minimally invasive procedures for effectively treating urinary stones and other obstructions.</p></p>
