<p><strong>The SUV Ignition Coil Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-spark</li><li>Multi-spark</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The SUV ignition coil market comprises two main types: single-spark and multi-spark. Single-spark coils provide one spark per engine cycle, suitable for standard fuel efficiency and performance needs. In contrast, multi-spark coils generate multiple sparks within a single cycle, enhancing combustion efficiency and power output, making them ideal for high-performance vehicles. Each type caters to different consumer preferences, with single-spark coils typically preferred for economy and reliability, while multi-spark coils appeal to enthusiasts seeking improved engine performance.</p></p>
