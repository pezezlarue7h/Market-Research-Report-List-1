<p><ul><li>Passive Pixel Sensor CMOS</li><li>Active Pixel Sensor CMOS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Backside Illuminated (BSI) CMOS sensor market comprises two primary types: Passive Pixel Sensor (PPS) CMOS and Active Pixel Sensor (APS) CMOS. PPS CMOS sensors utilize a simpler design, focusing on basic light detection, making them cost-effective for low-resolution applications. In contrast, APS CMOS sensors incorporate an active mechanism that enhances image quality and functionality, allowing for higher resolutions and advanced features like faster readout speeds. Both types are essential in various industries, including consumer electronics and automotive applications.</p></p>
