<p><strong>The Liquified Natural Gas Carrier Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Self-supporting Type</li><li>Film Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Liquified Natural Gas (LNG) carrier market is primarily categorized into two types: self-supporting and film type carriers. Self-supporting carriers utilize the ship's hull to support the LNG containment system, which features a prismatic shape allowing for efficient space utilization and stability. In contrast, film-type carriers employ membrane technology with thin-walled structures that are secured to the hull, enabling flexibility and lightweight design. Both types aim to ensure safe, efficient transport of LNG, catering to different operational needs and market demands.</p></p>
