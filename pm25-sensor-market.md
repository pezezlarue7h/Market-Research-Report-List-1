<p><p>The PM2.5 sensor market primarily includes two types: infrared sensors and laser sensors. Infrared sensors detect particulate matter by measuring the absorption of infrared light, offering a cost-effective solution for air quality monitoring. Laser sensors, on the other hand, utilize scattered light from laser beams to provide high accuracy and real-time data on PM2.5 concentrations. Both sensor types are crucial for environmental monitoring, industrial applications, and health assessments, catering to diverse needs in air quality management.</p></p>
