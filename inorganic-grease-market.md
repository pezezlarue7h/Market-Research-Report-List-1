<p><p>The inorganic grease market includes types such as bentonite grease and silicone grease, each catering to specific industrial applications. Bentonite grease, made from clay-based thickeners, excels in high-temperature and heavy-load environments, offering excellent stability and moisture resistance. Silicone grease, known for its versatility, provides superior thermal stability, chemical resistance, and low-temperature performance, making it ideal for use in diverse sectors such as automotive, electronics, and aerospace. Both types enhance lubrication performance under extreme conditions.</p></p>
