<p><p>The preclinical in-vivo imaging system market includes various advanced imaging technologies that facilitate research in disease mechanisms and therapy evaluations. Optical imaging systems utilize light to visualize biological processes; nuclear imaging systems employ radioactive tracers to assess physiological functions. Micro MRI systems provide high-resolution anatomical images, while micro CT systems offer detailed 3D imaging of small animals. Micro ultrasound systems deliver real-time imaging of soft tissues. Photoacoustic imaging combines optical and ultrasound data for enhanced visualization, while magnetic particle imaging emphasizes imaging with magnetic nanoparticles for specific applications.</p></p>
