<p><p>The flange gasket sheet market encompasses three primary types: metallic, semi-metallic, and non-metallic. Metallic gaskets, often made of steel or other metals, provide durability and high-temperature resistance, making them suitable for demanding environments. Semi-metallic gaskets combine metal and non-metal components to enhance flexibility and sealing capabilities, ideal for variable pressures. Non-metallic gaskets, typically composed of rubber or plastic, offer excellent chemical resistance and are lightweight, primarily used in low-pressure applications. Each type serves specific industry needs.</p></p>
