<p><strong>The Oil & Gas Centrifugal Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Axial Flow Pumps</li><li>Mixed/Radial Flow Pumps</li><li>Peripheral Pumps</li><li>Jet Pumps</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The oil and gas centrifugal pump market consists of various types, each designed for specific applications. Axial flow pumps move fluid along the pump axis, ideal for high flow rates with low pressure. Mixed/radial flow pumps combine axial and radial flow characteristics, suitable for diverse conditions. Peripheral pumps handle low flow rates at high pressure, commonly used in smaller applications. Jet pumps utilize a jet of fluid to create a vacuum, effectively lifting fluids from underground sources, especially in well applications.</p></p>
