<p><ul><li>Low Vacuum Valve</li><li>High Vacuum Valve</li><li>Ultra-high Vacuum Valve</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vacuum valves market is categorized into three types based on pressure ranges: low vacuum valves, high vacuum valves, and ultra-high vacuum valves. Low vacuum valves operate at pressures from atmospheric pressure down to approximately 1 mbar, suitable for general applications. High vacuum valves function in the range of 1 mbar to 10^-3 mbar, ideal for industrial and research environments. Ultra-high vacuum valves are designed for pressures below 10^-3 mbar, essential for advanced scientific applications like particle accelerators and semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>
