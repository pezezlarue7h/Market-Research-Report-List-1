<p><strong>The Meniscal Repair Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>All-inside Repair</li><li>Outside-in Repair</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The meniscal repair market is primarily segmented into two main types: all-inside repair and outside-in repair. All-inside repair techniques involve inserting sutures through mini-arthroscopic portals, allowing for precise repairs within the joint while minimizing soft tissue disruption. Conversely, outside-in repair techniques utilize an approach that allows surgeons to place sutures from outside the knee joint, providing access to larger tears. Both methods aim to restore meniscal function and promote healing, catering to varying injury types and surgical preferences.</p></p>
