<p><p>The car steering wheel market comprises two main types: Type I and Type II. In contrast, Type II encompasses advanced steering technologies, such as electronic or drive-by-wire systems, which enhance precision and enable features like automatic lane-keeping. Type II systems often incorporate sensors and algorithms for improved driver assistance, offering greater adaptability and connectivity in modern vehicles, thus transforming the driving experience.</p></p>
