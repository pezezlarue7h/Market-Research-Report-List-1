<p><p>The bitumen membranes market primarily includes three types: APP (Atactic Polypropylene), SBS (Styrene-Butadiene-Styrene), and SEBS (Styrene-Ethylene-Butylene-Styrene). APP membranes are heat-reflective and UV-resistant, making them suitable for hot climates. SBS membranes offer flexibility and durability, ideal for varying temperatures. SEBS membranes provide enhanced elasticity and weather resistance, catering to extreme conditions. Each type addresses specific application needs in roofing and waterproofing, contributing to the diverse preferences within the construction and civil engineering sectors.</p></p>
