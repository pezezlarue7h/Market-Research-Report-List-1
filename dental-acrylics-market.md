<p><strong>The Dental Acrylics Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Heat Cure Acrylic Resin</li><li>Self-Curing Acrylic Resin</li><li>Cold Cure Acrylic Resin</li><li>Light-Cured Acrylic Resin</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dental acrylic market consists of various types, each serving specific applications. Heat cure acrylic resin is preferred for denture fabrication due to its strength and durability. Self-curing acrylic resin polymerizes at room temperature, making it suitable for quick repairs. Cold cure acrylic resin, often used for orthodontic applications, hardens without heat, allowing for easy handling. Light-cured acrylic resin offers rapid setting times under ultraviolet light, enhancing precision in restorations. Other types encompass specialized formulations for diverse dental needs, ensuring versatility in treatments.</p></p>
