<p><ul><li>Active</li><li>Passive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The SUV speed sensor market is divided into two main types: active and passive sensors. Active sensors utilize external power sources to generate a signal that measures vehicle speed, providing more accurate and real-time data. In contrast, passive sensors rely on the movement of the vehicle to generate a signal without needing external power, making them simpler and more cost-effective. Both types play vital roles in vehicle performance, safety, and integrated driving systems, catering to different manufacturing preferences and consumer needs.</p></p>
