<p><strong>The Rubber Carbon Black Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermal Black</li><li>Furnace Black</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Rubber Carbon Black market primarily consists of two types: Thermal Black and Furnace Black. Thermal Black is produced through the thermal decomposition of hydrocarbons, resulting in larger particle sizes and lower reinforcement properties, making it suitable for specific applications like industrial rubber. In contrast, Furnace Black is created through the combustion of hydrocarbons in a controlled environment, yielding finer particles and superior reinforcing capabilities, making it more widely used in tires and high-performance rubber products.</p></p>
