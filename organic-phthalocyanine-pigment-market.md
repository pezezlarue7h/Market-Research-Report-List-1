<p><p>The organic phthalocyanine pigment market is primarily divided into two types: metal phthalocyanine pigments and metal-free phthalocyanine pigments. Metal phthalocyanine pigments contain metallic ions, which enhance their color strength and stability, making them suitable for various applications including paints and coatings. In contrast, metal-free phthalocyanine pigments are derived without metallic components, offering lower toxicity and environmental impact. Both types are valued for their vibrant colors, excellent lightfastness, and versatility across industries like textiles, plastics, and printing inks.</p></p>
