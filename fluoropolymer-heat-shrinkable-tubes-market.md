<p><p>Fluoropolymer heat shrinkable tubes are categorized based on their specific chemical compositions and properties. PFA (Perfluoroalkoxy) provides excellent chemical resistance and flexibility, making it ideal for varied applications. FEP (Fluorinated Ethylene Propylene) combines high thermal stability with ease of processing. PTFE (Polytetrafluoroethylene) is known for its exceptional non-stick characteristics and high-temperature tolerance. PVDF (Polyvinylidene Fluoride) offers good mechanical properties and UV resistance. Other types may include specialized blends or formulations tailored for specific industrial needs.</p></p>
