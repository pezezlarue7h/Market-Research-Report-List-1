<p><p>The pneumatic diaphragm valve market includes several key types. Normally Closed Type valves remain shut when not pressurized, preventing flow until activated. Normally Open Type valves allow flow by default, closing only when air pressure is applied. Reciprocating Type valves feature a moving diaphragm that enables precise flow control in varied applications. Each type caters to specific operational needs across industries, facilitating effective fluid control, safety, and efficiency in processes ranging from water treatment to chemical processing.</p></p>
