<p><strong>The Optical Line Protection Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>1+1 Optical Line Protection</li><li>1:1 Optical Line Protection</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Optical Line Protection ensures the reliability of optical networks by providing redundancy and minimizing service interruptions. The 1+1 Optical Line Protection configuration involves two parallel optical paths, where one serves as a backup to the primary line. In contrast, 1:1 Optical Line Protection uses a dedicated backup for each primary line, ensuring that each connection has its own protection scheme. Both types enhance network resilience, allowing for seamless data transmission even during outages or disruptions.</p></p>
