<p><p>Photovoltaic Noise Barriers (PVNB) integrate solar panels into noise barriers along highways and railways, serving dual functions of noise reduction and energy generation. The market can be segmented into conventional and bifacial types. Conventional PVNB utilizes traditional solar panels, while bifacial PVNB harnesses sunlight from both sides, enhancing efficiency in space-restricted environments. The bifacial market appeals to municipalities seeking higher energy yield and sustainability, while conventional models remain prevalent in established infrastructure projects focused on immediate noise mitigation and energy production.</p></p>
