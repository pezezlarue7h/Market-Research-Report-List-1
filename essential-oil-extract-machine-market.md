<p><p>The essential oil extraction machine market includes two primary types: hydraulic and screw oil press machines. Hydraulic type machines utilize high pressure to extract essential oils efficiently, ensuring minimal heat generation and preserving oil quality. In contrast, screw oil press machines employ a mechanical screw-driven mechanism to extract oils from plant materials, offering continuous processing and higher throughput. Both types are essential in various industries, including aromatherapy, cosmetics, and food, catering to diverse extraction needs and preferences.</p></p>
