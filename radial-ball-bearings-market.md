<p><ul><li>Conrad Type</li><li>Self-Aligning Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The radial ball bearings market features various types, including the Conrad type and self-aligning type. The Conrad type is designed for applications where axial loads are minimal, offering high-speed performance and compact design. Conversely, self-aligning ball bearings accommodate misalignment, making them ideal for applications subject to shaft deflection or mounting errors. Both types cater to diverse industrial sectors, enhancing operational efficiency and reliability in machinery by minimizing friction and wear, ensuring smooth rotational movement.</p></p>
